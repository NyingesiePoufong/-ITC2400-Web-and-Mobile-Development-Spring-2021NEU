Hard Boiled Eggs Recipe

Ingredients

  • Water
  • At least 2 or More Eggs

Steps To Prepare Eggs

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan. Cover eggs with enough water to fully submerge the eggs. If you put more than two eggs make sure there is an equal amount of space in-between each egg.
  2. Cover the sauce pan with a lid and boil the water with high heat on the stove for 12 minutes. If the water boils over. Remove the lid and keep boiling the eggs until 12 minutes have passed.
  3. Remove the hot water from the saucepan and cover the eggs with cold water until they are cool.
  4. Remove the eggshell by cracking and peeling the it.
