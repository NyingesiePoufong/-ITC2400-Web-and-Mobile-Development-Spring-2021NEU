My Favorite Three Album Releases

Nostalgia, Ultra
Nostalgia, Ultra was released in February of 2011 by Odd Future group memember, Frank Ocean. The album contains a total of fourteen tracks and was rated by Pitchfork Media with a critial reception score of 7.8/10. My personal favorite songs on the album are "Novacane", "Songs For Women", "Swim Good", "There Will Be Tears", and "Lovecrimes".

Channel Orange
Channel Orange was released in July of 2012 by Odd Future group memember, Frank Ocean. The album contains a total of seventeen tracks and was rated by Pitchfork Media with a critial reception score of 9.5/10. Channel Orange won the Grammy catergory of " Best Urban Contemporary Album" in 2013. I love the album as a whole but my personal favorite tracks are "Thinkin Bout You", "Pink Matter", "Pyramids", "Super Rich Kids", "Sierra Leone", "Lost" and "Forrest Gump".

Blonde
Blonde was released in August of 2016 by Odd Future group memember, Frank Ocean. The album contains a total of seventeen tracks and was rated by Pitchfork Media with a critial reception score of 9/10. The Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) certified Blonde as a platinum album. My personal favorite songs on the album are "Pink + White", "Nights", and "Ivy".
